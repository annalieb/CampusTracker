About

Campus Cares provides centralized information on COVID-19 test results at various colleges and universities in the Boston region. Users will gain awareness of COVID conditions at peer instutitions to better understand risk levels at their own campus.

To navigate the website, hover over the main drop-down menu and select the schools you would like to view
